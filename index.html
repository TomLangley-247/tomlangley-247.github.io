Welcome to Muscle Meals

We understand how challenging it can be to maintain a healthy, high-protein diet as a student. Balancing coursework, gym time, and a social life leaves little room for expensive, time-consuming meals. The struggle to stay on budget while making sure you’re getting enough protein to fuel your gym progress is real, and it can often feel overwhelming.

That’s where Muscle Meals comes in! Our mission is simple: to help you achieve your fitness goals without breaking the bank. We provide a collection of easy-to-make, high-protein recipes that won’t stretch your wallet. Whether you’re looking to bulk up, lean out, or just maintain a healthy diet, we’ve got recipes that are budget-friendly and packed with the protein your muscles need.

High Protein Chicken Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g chicken breast (cooked and diced)
  • 150g whole wheat pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1/2 cup low-fat Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup spinach (fresh or frozen)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the pasta according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the cooked and diced chicken breast to the pan, stir to combine, and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, then add the Greek yogurt and Parmesan cheese. Stir until the sauce becomes creamy.
  6. Add the spinach and stir until wilted (if using fresh) or heated through (if using frozen).
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat evenly with the sauce.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy your high-protein chicken pasta!

High Protein Stir Fry

Ingredients

  • 200g lean beef or chicken breast (sliced)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce (low-sodium)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 green bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (grated)
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha or chili sauce (optional for heat)
  • Cooked brown rice or quinoa for serving

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced meat and cook for 4-5 minutes until browned. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add the garlic and ginger. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add the vegetables (bell peppers, carrot, onion, broccoli) and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Return the cooked meat to the pan and stir in the soy sauce, sriracha (optional), and sesame seeds (optional).
  6. Cook for another 2 minutes, stirring to combine everything.
  7. Serve hot over cooked brown rice or quinoa for a protein-packed meal.

High Protein Chili

Ingredients

  • 500g diced chicken
  • 1 can black beans (drained and rinsed)
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (with juice)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened (about 5 minutes).
  3. Add the ground chicken and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the chili powder, cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), salt, and pepper.
  5. Add the diced tomatoes and black beans. Stir to combine.
  6. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  7. Serve hot with your choice of toppings like Greek yogurt, shredded cheese, or cilantro.

High Protein Greek Yogurt Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain, non-fat)
  • 1/2 cup almond milk or any milk of choice
  • 1 scoop vanilla whey protein powder
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 tablespoon peanut butter (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender, combine the Greek yogurt, almond milk, protein powder, banana, berries, and peanut butter (if using).
  2. Blend on high until smooth and creamy.
  3. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy your high-protein smoothie!
